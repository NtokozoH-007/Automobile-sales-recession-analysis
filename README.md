# Automobile Sales and Recession Impact Analysis

## Project Overview

This project investigates how economic recessions influence automobile sales, vehicle demand, advertising expenditure, consumer confidence, unemployment, and regional sales performance.

The analysis uses Python data visualization tools to compare automobile sales during recession and non-recession periods.

## Business Problem

Automobile companies operate in an industry that is highly sensitive to economic conditions. During recessions, consumers may delay purchasing vehicles because of reduced income, unemployment, lower confidence, and restricted access to credit.

This project aims to identify the areas of the automobile business that are most affected during economic downturns and provide insights that management can use when preparing for future recessions.

## Objectives

The analysis seeks to answer the following questions:

1. How have automobile sales changed over time?
2. How do recession-period sales compare with non-recession sales?
3. Which vehicle categories experience the largest decline during recessions?
4. What relationship exists between unemployment and automobile sales?
5. How does advertising expenditure change during recessions?
6. Which regions or offices generate the highest sales during recession periods?
7. What business actions could reduce the impact of future economic downturns?
