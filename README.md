# 🚗 Automobile Sales and Recession Impact Analysis

## Business Intelligence Portfolio Project

**Author:** Ntokozo Hlongwane

---

## Project Overview

Economic recessions significantly influence consumer spending and purchasing behaviour, particularly within the automobile industry. During periods of economic uncertainty, changes in employment, consumer confidence, and disposable income can directly affect vehicle demand and business performance.

This project investigates how automobile sales change during recession and non-recession periods using Python for data analysis and visualization. The objective is to identify key business trends and provide data-driven recommendations that could help an automobile company prepare for future economic downturns.

This project demonstrates practical skills in exploratory data analysis (EDA), business intelligence, and data visualization using industry-standard Python libraries.

---

## Business Problem

Automobile manufacturers operate in a highly competitive industry that is heavily influenced by macroeconomic conditions. Understanding how recessions affect vehicle sales, advertising expenditure, and customer purchasing behaviour enables organizations to make better strategic decisions.

The goal of this analysis is to identify patterns in historical automobile sales data and uncover actionable business insights.

---

## Business Questions

This project seeks to answer the following questions:

- How have automobile sales changed over time?
- How do recession periods compare with non-recession periods?
- Which vehicle categories experience the greatest decline during recessions?
- What relationship exists between unemployment and vehicle sales?
- How does advertising expenditure change during economic downturns?
- Which company offices or regions generate the highest sales during recessions?
- What strategies can businesses implement to reduce the impact of future recessions?
